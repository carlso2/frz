Ready to Fight?

Nunchucks doesn't warm up. Neither should you.

Use ←/→ to close distance — punches only land within range. Hold DUCK or tap JUMP (L / ↑) to dodge — jumping escapes any punch, even mid-air. If there's room past Nunchucks, Logan leaps clean over him and turns to keep fighting; if not, he hops backward instead. Tap PUNCH to hit back. Rough Nunchucks up twice and he'll start dancing — rapid retreats followed by surprise rushes. Land 3 hits on him in a row and he unlocks his own jump escape, over or backward, just like you. First fighter to take 30 punches loses.
